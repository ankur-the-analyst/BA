📋 Executive Summary

🎯 Project Overview

This BRD outlines the comprehensive automation strategy for a startup coffee shop, focusing on streamlining operations, enhancing customer experience, and establishing scalable systems for future growth. The automation initiative covers POS systems, inventory management, customer relationship management, staff scheduling, and data analytics.

💼 Business Objectives

  • Increase operational efficiency by 35%
  • Reduce manual errors by 80%
  • Improve customer satisfaction scores to 4.5+
  • Establish data-driven decision making
  • Create scalable foundation for multi-location expansion

🚧 Business Problem Statement

The coffee shop currently relies on manual processes that result in inefficiencies, high labor costs, inaccurate inventory management, and poor customer data utilization. There is a need to automate operations to ensure scalability, accuracy, and a better customer experience.

🧭 Scope of Work

✅ In Scope

  • POS System Implementation
  • CRM and Loyalty Program
  • Inventory and Staff Management
  • Online Ordering & Customer Notifications
  • Sales Analytics & Reporting

❌ Out of Scope

  • Franchise Expansion Planning
  • Hardware Procurement
  • HR Policy Formulation
  • Vendor Legal Agreements

⚙️ Functional Requirements

🛒 Point of Sale (POS) System

  • Process orders and payments
  • Handle multiple payment methods
  • Generate receipts and invoices
  • Track sales in real-time
  • Support split payments
  • Offline capability during outages

📦 Inventory Management

  • Real-time stock tracking
  • Automated reorder alerts
  • Supplier integration
  • Waste tracking and reporting
  • Cost analysis per item
  • Expiration date monitoring

👨‍💼 Customer Relationship Management

  • Customer database management
  • Loyalty program automation
  • Personalized promotions
  • Email marketing campaigns
  • Customer feedback collection
  • Purchase history tracking

📱 Online Ordering System

  • Mobile-friendly ordering interface
  • Real-time menu updates
  • Order scheduling and pickup times
  • Integration with POS system
  • Customer notification system
  • Delivery tracking (if applicable)

👥 Staff Management

  • Automated shift scheduling
  • Time tracking and attendance
  • Payroll integration
  • Performance monitoring
  • Training module tracking
  • Communication tools

📊 Analytics & Reporting

  • Sales performance dashboards
  • Customer behavior analytics
  • Inventory turnover reports
  • Financial summaries
  • Peak hour analysis
  • Predictive forecasting

🧩 Non-Functional Requirements

  • System uptime ≥ 99.5%
  • Daily data backups
  • Interface load time ≤ 2 seconds
  • Full mobile compatibility
  • Scalability for multi-branch support
  • Compliance with data protection laws (e.g., GDPR)
